My Four-Year Journey with VEX Robotics

From my first robot build to championship victories, here's how robotics shaped my problem-solving skills and passion for engineering.

It all started in Grade 4 when I first laid eyes on a VEX robot. The intricate mechanisms, the whirring motors, and the endless possibilities captivated me instantly. Little did I know that this moment would define the next four years of my life.

The Beginning

My first robot was a simple chassis with basic wheels and a claw. It could barely move in a straight line, but to me, it was perfect. I spent countless hours in the robotics lab, tweaking the design, learning to code, and understanding the fundamentals of mechanical engineering.

The early days were filled with failures. Motors burned out, gears stripped, and programs crashed. But each failure taught me something new. I learned about torque ratios, gear trains, and the importance of precision in engineering.

The Breakthrough

Everything changed in Grade 6 when I decided to tackle autonomous programming. I taught myself advanced mathematics—trigonometry, calculus concepts, and PID control algorithms—all to make my robot move more precisely.

"The robot doesn't care how many hours you've put in. It only cares about the quality of your code and the precision of your build."

This mindset pushed me to develop efficient control systems that would later earn me the Best Software Programming Award at the VEX Asia Open. The feeling of seeing my robot execute a perfect autonomous routine was indescribable.

Championship Glory

The pinnacle of my journey came at the 2025 "Nangong Cup" VEX Elite Invitational. After months of preparation, countless late nights, and endless iterations, my team emerged as champions. Standing on that podium, holding the trophy, I realized how far I had come.

But more than the victories, robotics taught me valuable life skills: teamwork under pressure, creative problem-solving, and the resilience to keep going when things don't work the first time.

Looking Forward

As I continue my journey, I'm exploring new frontiers in robotics—AI integration, computer vision, and advanced control systems. The foundation I built through VEX robotics has prepared me for whatever challenges lie ahead.

To anyone considering joining robotics: do it. The journey is challenging, frustrating at times, but incredibly rewarding. You'll learn more about yourself than you ever thought possible.

Mechanical Design Portfolio

Collection of CAD designs and mechanical structures for various robotics challenges.

Overview

This portfolio showcases my mechanical design work for VEX robotics competitions. Each design was created to solve specific challenges, from lifting mechanisms to drive train optimizations.

Design Philosophy

Simplicity First

The best designs are often the simplest. Fewer parts mean fewer points of failure.

Iterative Design

Each design goes through multiple iterations based on testing and competition feedback.

CAD Modeling

All designs are created in CAD software for precise measurements and simulations.

Featured Designs

The portfolio includes lift mechanisms, intake systems, drive trains, and specialized tools for specific game challenges. Each design balances speed, strength, and reliability.
